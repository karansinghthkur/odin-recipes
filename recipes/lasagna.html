Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy cheese. This hearty and comforting meal is perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Brown the ground beef with onions and garlic.
  3. Add tomato sauce and seasonings to the meat mixture.
  4. Mix ricotta cheese with eggs and Parmesan.
  5. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese mixture in a baking dish.
  6. Top with mozzarella cheese.
  7. Bake at 375Â°F for 25 minutes.
  8. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
