Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

Chicken Tikka Masala is a popular Indian curry dish featuring tender chunks of marinated chicken in a rich, creamy tomato-based sauce. This flavorful meal is typically served with rice or naan bread.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Yogurt
  • Lemon juice
  • Spices (garam masala, turmeric, cumin)
  • Onion
  • Garlic and ginger
  • Tomato puree
  • Heavy cream
  • Butter
  • Fresh cilantro

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices for at least 1 hour.
  2. Grill or bake the marinated chicken until cooked through.
  3. In a large pan, saut√© onions, garlic, and ginger.
  4. Add tomato puree and spices, simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in cream and butter.
  6. Add the cooked chicken to the sauce.
  7. Simmer for 5-10 minutes until heated through.
  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with rice or naan.
