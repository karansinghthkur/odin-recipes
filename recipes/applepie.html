Apple Pie

Description

Apple Pie is a classic American dessert featuring a flaky crust filled with sweet and spiced apple slices. This comforting treat is perfect for holidays, special occasions, or as a delightful end to any meal.

Ingredients

  • Pie crust (homemade or store-bought)
  • Apples (Granny Smith or other tart varieties)
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Nutmeg
  • Lemon juice
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Egg (for egg wash)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Peel, core, and slice apples.
  3. Mix apples with sugar, spices, lemon juice, and flour.
  4. Line a pie dish with one pie crust.
  5. Fill with apple mixture and dot with butter.
  6. Cover with the second pie crust, seal edges, and cut vents.
  7. Brush with egg wash and sprinkle with sugar.
  8. Bake for 45-50 minutes until golden brown.
  9. Cool before serving.
